Baked Ziti

Baked ziti was a comfort food for me growing up, and a staple throughout college. Cheap, easy, warm, filling, this dish had it all.

I hope you enjoy my baked ziti as much as I do!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz ziti (1 box)
  • 12 oz tomato sauce (1.5 jars)
  • 16 oz Italian sausage
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 4 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Boil water and cook pasta 10-12 minutes. Drain.
  3. Brown sausage in frying pan.
  4. In a bowl, combine ricotta, herbs, garlic, egg, salt and pepper.
  5. Once the pasta and sausage are cooked, combine sausage, pasta, and tomato sauce in a pot. Transfer to a baking dish.
  6. Spread the ricotta mixture throughout the dish by adding big clumps and lightly swirling them.
  7. Cover the dish with the shredded mozzarella.
  8. Bake in oven for 20 mins or until cheese is brown and bubbly. Enjoy!
