Mac and Cheese

When I first found this recipe, I knew it could be better. I have made this dish numerous times, each one trying something new. This went on to find the dish you see here.

I hope you enjoy my homemade mac and cheese recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups of milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • red chili flakes
  • 16 oz elbow macaroni (1 box)
  • 16 oz sharp cheddar cheese
  • 8 oz American cheese
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Saute onion and bay leaf for 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in flour and cook 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  4. Stir in milk, salt, mustard and pepper; cook, stirring frequently, until mixture boils and thickens.
  5. Meanwhile, bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  6. To the milk mixture add the Cheddar and American cheeses; stir until cheese melts. Remove bay leaf.
  7. Combine macaroni and cheese sauce in a baking dish.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until hot and bubbly. Let cool 10 minutes before serving.
  9. Add red chili flakes to the top if desired. Enjoy!
